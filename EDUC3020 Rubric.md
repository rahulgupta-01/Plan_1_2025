# Task 1 Marking Rubric

## **1. Understanding & Justification of Strategies (40%)**

**HD+**  
Excellent explanation of highly appropriate HIT strategies linked to specific syllabus outcomes. A very strong justification supported by **4+ credible references** integrated into discussion.

**HD**  
Comprehensive explanation of both strategies, explicitly linked to syllabus outcomes. Strong justification supported by **4+ credible references** integrated into discussion.

**D**  
Clear explanation of both strategies with syllabus links. Justification supported by **3–4 references**.

**C**  
Adequate explanation with some syllabus links. Justification supported by **2–3 references**.

**P**  
Basic explanation; minimal syllabus links. Justification supported by **1–2 references**.

**MF**  
Basic explanation; minimal syllabus links. Justification is inadequate or too weak even though supported by **1–2 references**.

**F**  
Strategies unclear or unrelated to syllabus. No justification or references.

**N/A**  
No attempt / unauthorised use of AI

---

## **2. Instructional Examples & Classroom Illustration (40%)**

**HD+**  
Provides **2+ highly detailed** instructional examples per appropriate HIT strategy, very clearly showing classroom implementation and adjustments for diverse learners (e.g., NCCD, cultural diversity) in a senior class context.

**HD**  
Provides **2+ detailed** instructional examples per strategy, clearly showing classroom implementation and adjustments for diverse learners (e.g., NCCD, cultural diversity).

**D**  
Provides at least **1 detailed example per strategy**, showing classroom implementation and some adjustments for diverse learners.

**C**  
Provides general examples with adequate detail; mention of adjustments could be clearer to illustrate implementation for diverse learners.

**P**  
Provides general examples with basic details; minimal mention of adjustments for diverse learners.

**MF**  
Provides general examples with limited details; inadequate mention of adjustments for diverse learners.

**F**  
Provides vague or incomplete examples; no clear adjustments. No examples provided.

**N/A**  
No attempt / unauthorised use of AI or the work of others

---

## **3. Structure & Proposal Format (10%)**

**HD+**  
Exact structure (intro, two strategies, conclusion). Writing is very concise, logical, clear and very close to word limit.

**HD**  
Follows proposal structure (intro, two strategies, conclusion). Writing is concise, logical, clear and close to word limit.

**D**  
Follows proposal structure (intro, two strategies, conclusion). Writing is logical and within word limits ±5%.

**C**  
Mostly follows structure; minor lapses in flow or clarity. ±10% word limit.

**P**  
Structure evident but inconsistent; exceeds/under word limit by 20%.

**MF**  
Limited structure; exceeds/under word limit by 30%.

**F**  
No clear structure; significantly outside word limit.

**N/A**  
No attempt / unauthorised use of AI or the work of others

---

## **4. Academic Writing & Referencing (10%)**

**HD+**  
APA 7th referencing accurate throughout; all sources cited correctly in-text and reference list.

**HD**  
APA 7th referencing accurate throughout; all sources cited correctly in-text and most of the reference list.

**D**  
APA 7th referencing mostly accurate throughout; most sources cited correctly in-text and most of the reference list.

**C**  
A few minor errors in APA referencing; sources mostly cited correctly.

**P**  
A number of minor errors or a major error in APA referencing; sources mostly cited correctly.

**MF**  
Several APA errors; incomplete citations.

**F**  
Attempted APA referencing but inaccurate or incomplete.

**N/A**  
No referencing provided.
